From Blue Walls to White Silence

Chefchaouen · 2007

This series emerged from an extended walk through Chefchaouen, a city defined by an overwhelming chromatic presence. During the artist’s stay, it had not snowed in the region for seventeen years, reinforcing a sense of temporal suspension.

As the sequence unfolds, colour dissolves into white, shifting perception from saturation toward silence.
